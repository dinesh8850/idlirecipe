Idli

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups idli rice (parboiled rice)
  • 1/2 cup whole urad dal (black gram lentil)
  • 1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds (methi seeds)
  • Salt, to taste
  • Water, as needed
  • or ghee, for greasing the idli plates

Instructions:

  1. Wash the rice and urad dal separately and soak them in water overnight. Add fenugreek seeds to the urad dal while soaking.
  2. Drain the water from the rice and grind it to a smooth batter consistency using a wet grinder or a food processor. Use water sparingly while grinding.
  3. Drain the water from the urad dal and grind it to a smooth and fluffy consistency using a wet grinder or a food processor. Use water sparingly while grinding.
  4. Mix the rice and urad dal batter together in a large mixing bowl. Add salt to taste and mix well.
  5. Cover the bowl and let the batter ferment overnight or for 8-10 hours in a warm place. The batter should double in volume and become slightly sour.
  6. Grease the idli plates with oil or ghee.
  7. Pour the batter into each of the idli molds and steam for 10-12 minutes or until the idlis are cooked through.
  8. Once the idlis are cooked, remove them from the steamer and let them cool for a few minutes.
  9. Use a knife or a spoon to gently remove the idlis from the molds.
  10. Serve hot with chutney, sambar or any curry of your choice.
